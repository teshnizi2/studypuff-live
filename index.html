FAQ

Gentle answers to common questions.

Can't find yours? Write to us — we answer within 3 workdays.

Are the livestreams really free? +

Yes — always. Three live sessions a week on YouTube and Twitch, no sign-up required. You'll only see a workshop mentioned if we genuinely think it will help you.

Who is StudyPuff for? +

Students of any age — high school, university, adult learners — especially those who find traditional productivity culture loud, stressful, or hustle-y.

What makes the workshops "science-based"? +

Every module is grounded in peer-reviewed research on attention, sleep, spaced repetition and motivation — and adapted for real student life, not lab conditions.

Can I join from outside Europe? +

Absolutely. About 40% of our community lives outside Europe. Livestreams are recorded and workshop replays are available for 12 months.

What if I can't afford a paid workshop? +

Every cohort has scholarship seats. Fill out the form on the contact page — no questions, no paperwork beyond a two-line message.
