HI

What we believe

Four gentle principles.

01

Rest is part of the work.

Sleep, breaks and play aren't "after the real work" — they are the real work.

02

Research over rah-rah.

Everything we teach is grounded in peer-reviewed science, adapted for real student life.

03

Small over scale.

Cohorts of 30 max, one email a week, and no ads. Small and good over big and loud.

04

Kindness is a strategy.

Self-compassion outperforms self-criticism in the long run. Our tools reflect that.
